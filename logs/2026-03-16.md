<img width="1440" height="900" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-16 at 7 42 16 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2da35148-2192-4188-aaf2-76fbf449fea9" />
Calculated the total acceleration magnitude with A_total = sqrt(ax² + ay² + az²)/16384 to make the detection independent of bar tilt. Smoothed the values using a low-pass filter smoothedA = alpha * smoothedA + (1 - alpha) * A_total to reduce noise. Implemented rep detection by setting thresholds: a rep starts when smoothedA > 1.05 (bar going down) and ends when smoothedA < 0.95 (bar coming up), with a debounce of 600 ms to prevent double counting. Added rep speed measurement by recording the timestamps of down and up movements and calculating repDuration = (upTime - downTime)/1000 seconds. The final program reliably counts reps, measures speed, and handles small bar rotations without crashing.
