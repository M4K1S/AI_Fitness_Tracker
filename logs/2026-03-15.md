March 15:
Started brainstorming ideas for the project and I settled on the idea of it being a barbell collar and to maybe add a clip to clip onto your clothes if doing bodyweight exercises. For parts will be using ESP32 (maybe use ESP32 pico later on because it’s more compact), MPU6050 module for accelerometer and gyroscope, and a small rechargeable battery (3.7 500mAh or 800mAh LiPo battery using a TP4056 module for charging). For the app interface I decided to use Flutter.
